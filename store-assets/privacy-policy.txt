Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2023

QR Code Assistant respects and protects the privacy of all users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection
We do not collect any personal information. All QR code generation and scanning operations are performed locally in your browser.

2. Permission Usage
- activeTab: Used to get the URL of the current tab
- storage: Used to save QR code scanning history
- tabs: Used to read current tab information

3. Data Storage
- All data is stored only in your local browser
- You can clear history records at any time

4. Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party services and does not transmit any data to external servers.

5. Updates
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published in the Chrome Web Store.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the feedback feature in the Chrome Web Store.
